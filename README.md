# *Arithmetical* for iOS

**Arithmetical** is a math app built for students. The application's games promote memorization and retention of important mathematical concepts that will continue to appear in future studies. Whether you look to become faster at converting 1101 (base 2) to 13 (base 10), factoring 315 into primes, recalling where the 5Ï€/3 lies on the unit circle, or finding the sum of 19 + 23 quickly, **Arithmetical** can help you reach your goals! 

Fully functional games include:

- Single Digit Addition
- Two Digit Addition
- Single Digit Multiplication
- Binary to Decimal Conversion
- Prime Factorization
- Unit Circle: Radians

Upcoming games:

- Trigonometric identities
- Complex numbers to polar/exponential form
- Matrix games
- Modular arithmetic games
- Fibonacci numbers
- Pi memorization
